Principles

At Sierra Park, we believe we can help make the world a better place. We strive to provide a collaborative school community where high expectations for student learning are recognized. Our learning environment encourages rigorous standarts, supports all learners, and nurtures an appreciation of both our national and our multicultural heritage. All students are focused on developing and sharing their special talents.

Purpose

For Sierra Park Elementary, the world would be a better place if students were able to explore their talents, while uplifting each other and their community.

Position Statement

For parents of El Sereno, who want the best education for their child, Sierra Park Elementary is a public school that is dedicated to empowering each child’s learning prowess and heritage. Unlike other schools that don’t focus on diversity, Sierra Park Elementary offers support and community para todos.

Personality

If Sierra Park was a person, he’d be a first generation teacher, who wants to work to preserve the culture of El Sereno. He’s family-oriented, and in his 30s. He also plays bass in a band, and they will play at weddings and at quinceaneras. He drives a Nissan 90s, he’s saving up for a better car. He has no loans from school, due to the various scholarships that he won. He’s also very resourceful, meaning that he is fiscally smart, and if his neighbor had a lemon tree, he wouldn’t be opposed to taking a few for himself. His friends describe him as charming, and socially outgoing; he knows how to demand attention and control a crowd in a room.

Logo

Overview

A new refined mark that cements Sierra Park as a proud, multicultural and modern school, instantly recognizable at any size.

Logo Variations

Stacked Logo

This logo is ideally used for documents or environments that require a large and visible logo. This logo is best used for embroidered shirts, or printed as a visual.

Centered Wordmark

This is an alternative to the main wordmark, and can be used where centered type is required. Can also be used for official documents such as report cards and flyers sent home if header space allows for a centered wordmark.

Horizontal Logo

This logo lockup is best used for as a header for internal office documents, and any paperwork that requires a small shark icon.

Main Wordmark

The main wordmark is used when the logo is so small that the shark gets lost. Recommended usage for this wordmark is on tags, paperwork, and anywhere that requires a small and instant read of the brand.

Clearspace

Clearspace around the logo is the same as the full height of the capital S.

Scale

Our logo is designed to be legible small, but we recommend not making it smaller than 1in / 72px tall. Usage for the smallest logo can include but isn't limited to: business cards, school IDs, shark tickets, etc.

Logo Use

Don't stretch or skew logo.

Don't change colors of logo.

Don't rotate or add additional adornments/items too close to the logo.

Color

Overview

The colors chosen were inspired by traditional Aztec colors, but tailored to fit the modern approach Sierra Park takes with its students. Broken down into a primary and secondary color palette, they can fit the different needs of an elementary school. The primary palette will be used across the board as the base and the secondary palette will be used for pops of color to highlight important aspects. The blues are often associated with trust and familiarity, something that is significant to the community of El Sereno, while also bringing the ocean motif together with the shark mascot. The secondary palette is reinforcing the confidence and determination found in the students and staff of Sierra Park Elementary with vibrancy, energy, and warmth. The orange found in the secondary palette will be used to signify community throughout Sierra Park’s branding.

Voice and Tone

Overview

Sierra Park is more innovative than it’s outdated appearance. Its tone of voice will help cement Sierra Park's brand identity as well as communicate as clearly as possible. We wanted to keep the spirit alive of El Sereno’s heritage and community alive while ackwoledging the changing demographics. The result is a clear, confident, approachable entity that is the new Sierra Park.

Core

Personable

Student-first language that represents current and changing demographics of the surrounding area to transform and unite our voice.

Direct

Clear communication that isn't too technical or jargon-y in order to be accessible and understood by a diverse range of parents, students, and faculty.

Consistent

Consistency breeds trust and reliability. Sierra Park is already reliable and known for their long history, so we want to show an elevated version of this reliability.
